## 📱 What is AgriMateKE?

AgriMateKE is a digital farming companion built specifically for Kenyan farmers. It helps smallholder farmers manage their daily farm operations — from tracking crops and livestock to recording sales, getting AI-powered advice, and connecting with veterinarians — all in one place.

Built with accessibility at its core: works on any phone browser, supports English and Kiswahili, and designed for farmers in both urban and rural Kenya.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌿 **Crop Tracking** — Growth stages, harvest dates, plot sizes with visual progress
- 🐄 **Livestock Records** — Daily production recording for poultry, dairy, goats, pigs
- 💰 **Sales & Expenses** — Full P&L tracking with profit margin calculation
- 🤖 **Shamba Bot** — AI farming assistant for crop advice, market prices, disease treatment
- 💹 **Market Prices** — Live prices from Nairobi, Nakuru, Mombasa with trend indicators
- 🌦️ **Weather & Advisory** — 7-day forecast with farming-specific alerts
- 🏥 **Vet Directory** — Verified vets with call, message, and farm visit booking
- 📋 **Govt Grants Board** — Subsidies, loans, and training programs
- 🛒 **Agri Deals** — Controlled ads from verified agro-companies
- ✅ **Tasks & Reminders** — Priority tasks with overdue detection
- 🌍 **Bilingual** — Full English and Kiswahili support
